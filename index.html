3D Vision Lab.

Lab. Information:

Room B235, School of Artifical Intelligence and Computer Science,   Jiangnan University, Wuxi, Jiangsu Province, China.

Our research interests inclue 3D vision, stereo image and light field image processing, image restoration and enhancement, object detection and tracking, deep learning.

We also focus on industry defect detection and other machine vision tasks (systems) for engineering applications of computer vision and artifical intelligence technologies.
