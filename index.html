3D Vision Research

Lab. Information:

School of Artifical Intelligence and Computer Science,   Jiangnan University, Wuxi, Jiangsu Province, PR. China, 214122.

Our research interests inclue 3D vision, image editing, stereo image processing, light field image processing, image restoration and enhancement, target detection and tracking, deep learning and etc.

We also focus on defect detection，video surveillance and other computer (machine) vision applications.
